About Us

Bella Vita is a natural skincare and fragrance brand based in Gurugram, India that was founded in 2018 by Anju and Aashima Anand, the mother and sister of Aakash Anand. The brand's products are organic, cruelty-free, and chemical-free, and are inspired by natural elements and ancient beauty practices. Bella Vita's best-selling products include: Nicolips Lip Lightening Scrub Lip Balm GrowBrow Eye Brows EyeLash Hair Growth and Volume Oil Eyelift Under Eye Gel Cream Nailstrong Oil For Cuticle Care, Nail Growth & Strength Bella Vita began with four products in the face and hair care line, and has since expanded to skincare, hair care, body care, and perfumes. The brand uses perfume oils imported from France, Italy, and Spain, and bottles its perfumes in India.
